Who We Are

Built for High-Stakes Environments

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber, and technology risk roles within regulated and high-consequence sectors.

Unlike traditional academic programmes, IDR operates at the intersection of university-level research and real-world practice. We partner with leading UK universities to develop curricula grounded in current threat landscapes — from AI governance and data sovereignty to operational resilience and regulatory compliance.

Our graduates don't just learn frameworks — they apply them. Through live projects with partner organisations, IDR practitioners enter the workforce deployment-ready, holding both academic credentials and verifiable industry experience.
